What is FinOps?

FinOps (Cloud Financial Operations) is a discipline and operating model that helps organizations manage cloud spend through visibility, allocation, optimization, and shared accountability across engineering, finance, and leadership.

Why cloud costs become unpredictable

  • Elasticity: spend changes with traffic, scaling, and experiments.
  • Shared ownership: many teams create resources, but no one owns the bill.
  • Hidden drivers: storage growth, data transfer, logs, and idle resources.
  • Complex pricing: discounts, commitments, and cross-service dependencies.

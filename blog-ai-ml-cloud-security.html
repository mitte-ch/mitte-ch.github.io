Attackers may attempt to evade detection or poison data. Mitigate by separating training pipelines, validating data sources, and keeping humans in the loop for high-impact actions.

  2. Instrument: ensure high-quality logs and consistent schemas.
  3. Baseline: start with rule-based detections, then add ML to improve precision.
  4. Human-in-the-loop: require confirmation for destructive actions.
  5. Measure: track precision, recall, time-to-detect (TTD), and time-to-respond (TTR).
